Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: May 2025

SwiftDesk ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. By using SwiftDesk, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect your name, email address, and a hashed version of your password. We never store your password in plain text.

Usage Data

We track how many times you use our AI tools (usage count) to enforce plan limits. We do not store the content of your AI-generated outputs beyond the immediate session.

Billing Information

Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. SwiftDesk stores only your Stripe customer ID and subscription status. We never see or store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details.

Content You Submit

When you use a SwiftDesk tool, you may paste clinical notes, billing information, or other data into our forms. This content is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. We do not permanently store the text you submit to tools. See Section 4 for details on how Anthropic processes this data.

Technical Data

We may collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and timestamps for security monitoring and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the SwiftDesk service
  • To authenticate your account and enforce plan limits
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To send account-related communications (e.g., subscription confirmations)
  • To improve our platform based on aggregate usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. User passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage. Access to user data is restricted to authorized systems only.

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at privacy@swiftdesk.io.

4. Third-Party Services

Anthropic (Claude AI)

Content you submit to SwiftDesk tools is processed by Anthropic's Claude API. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how they handle API request data. We recommend not submitting fully identified patient health information (PHI) through our tools unless you are covered by a Business Associate Agreement (BAA). See our HIPAA Notice for details.

Stripe

All payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. Stripe's privacy policy governs how they handle payment data. SwiftDesk receives only subscription status and customer identifiers from Stripe.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Tool submission content is not retained after the AI response is generated.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@swiftdesk.io. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

SwiftDesk is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of SwiftDesk after a policy change constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@swiftdesk.io.
